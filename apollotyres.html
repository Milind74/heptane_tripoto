After a rather inactive year gone by, most of us are itching to get back to travelling to create precious memories. Of course, many of us did make most of the travel relaxations and ventured out for short trips or longer workations even during the pandemic, coming away with unique experiences. Whether it is capturing the emotion of the journey, a drive through a snow-covered road, discovering hidden eateries or just a simple act of kindness that made our travel special – all of it makes for a lifetime of great memories. What if you could get rewarded for all these special travel moments that you’ve collected? Yes, you heard that right!

Road Buddies Season 2.0, powered by Apollo Apterra Tyres presents to you an opportunity of a lifetime. Share your best travel moments that reflect your passion for traveling, and get a chance to win an all new Royal Enfield Meteor bike, courtesy Apollo Tyres. Get ready to Unlock Your Spirit.

What is Bad Road Buddies 2.0?

After a successful season 1, Bad Road Buddies is back, this time bigger and better! Bringing to life the very passion for travel, the contest is giving you an opportunity to showcase your best travel experience.

So, for all the moments you’ve captured while travelling, Bad Road Buddies Season 2.0 is the most exciting way to draw on, as you get to choose from a wide range of themes to share your memories from:

1. Driving in the snow

If you have gone on a road trip and driven through a snow-covered road and crossed some of the most daunting terrains to drive on, then here is a chance to share that special memory.

2. Capturing the emotion of the journey

Share your best travel memories, capturing the location and the emotion.

3. Making a difference

A simple act of kindness, especially on the roads, goes a long way in creating a beautiful memory. Share your stories where you made a difference with that little act of kindness.

4. Video journaling

All vloggers come forward! Vlogging is an effortless way of preserving those travel memories. Share your best travel memories in an interesting vlog

5. A gastronomic quest

Good food is an integral part of a good trip. More often than not, the best eateries are either less explored or hidden.Bring out your stories of such interesting gastronomical quests from your travels.

How can you participate?

● Register on the Bad Road Buddies 2.0 website : www.badroadbuddies.com

● Share your best travel stories from any of the above-mentioned themes on your social media channels – Instagram, Facebook or Twitter, with an appropriate caption telling us the story.

● These posts could be either photographs or videos.

And, There’s More - A Wild Card Entry

If you in the entry on every platform - Instagram, Facebook and Twitter, that complies with all the requirements of the contest -

● Good quality entry - Images / Video

● Contest hashtag and @apollotyres to be tagged

What’s up for grabs?

The winner of the grand prize gets an all new Royal Enfield Meteor bike, courtesy Apollo Tyres. Not just that, there are a ton of other fabulous prizes including a Taj Safari voucher, DSLR cameras, iPhone 12, GoPros, Amazon Vouchers and more to be won from Apollo Tyres

How will the selection be done?

● Your entry’s eligibility for the selection round will be based on the engagement (likes/comments/shares) it receives.

● The entries with the highest likes or views will be shortlisted and selected by an expert jury panel

● The 40 best entries selected by the jury will be uploaded on the Bad Road Buddies website.

● On the site, the selected 40 entries will be available to people to vote for.

● You can spread the word to your friends and family, inviting them to vote for the entries.

● After accounting for the votes received, the top 10 entries will compete for the grand prize.

● The post with the highest number of votes will be declared the winner of the grand prize.

What are the contest dates?

● The Bad Road Buddies contest starts from 20th March 2021 and runs till 30th April 2021.

● All your entries should be sent between 20th March 2021 and 30th April 2021, so make sure you turn in your entries on time!

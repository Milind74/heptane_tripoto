Overview

Shimla is the most stunning and breathtaking valleys of Himalayas. Once Shimla was the go-to getaway paradise of British East India Company during the summer. The valley is set among the towering snow-capped Shivalik Mountains. Manali is another hotspot for tourists who get enthralled by the natural beauty with the mighty Himalayas in the backdrop. You can always enjoy the feeling of floating on the clouds. Check out this detailed itinerary below for more information.

Still Waiting to Travel? Make Mumbai Your Getaway with Staycations by Marriott International Hotels!

By Rajat kamboj
Mumbai

Months of staying at home and not being able to travel is a harsh reality that we had to face in 2020. While some of us started travelling again, others are yet to take the leap. Are your travel memories also catching rust because of the pandemic? With things slowly transitioning towards normalcy, I’m sure you're also looking for an invigorating getaway with assured safety.

What’s the easiest way to enjoy a relaxing getaway?

The new trend of vacationing that’s caught the travel community by storm is to make your own city a getaway. If you live in Mumbai, you enjoy the privilege of memory-making vacations just by being a local in the city of dreams; spectacular travel adventures are never too far away from you. Still wondering how? With luxurious staycations by Marriott International Hotels at your doorstep, a memorable holiday is always a hop, skip and jump away.

You can now experience the ideal getaway in Mumbai with exquisite staycations on offer by Marriott International Hotels. With several extravagant hotels spread across the city of dreams, you can indulge in some self-care, and treat yourself to a much-deserved break without leaving town.

Ideal for

Staycations that you can book with Marriott Bonvoy’s suite of offerings are tailor-made for anyone in Mumbai or Pune who is looking for a quick break from their daily routine without roaming too far away. You can enjoy this experience by yourself, as a couple or with your family. Since these properties are not far off from home, you can relish a mini vacation over a weekend. You also have the option of planning a lavish workcation at these hotels, a getaway best suited for business travelers.

Here are some recommended hotels that you can choose for your next staycation:

The St. Regis Mumbai

Promising an indulgent staycation with traditional elegance and modern touch, The St. Regis Mumbai boasts a plethora of indulgent amenities like an on-call butler service, traditional English teas, champagne sabering ceremonies and an exquisite spa. Enjoy a chef-curated meal at a select specialty venue for an extravagant dinner and breakfast.

JW Marriott Mumbai Juhu

Located along Juhu Beach, JW Marriott Mumbai Juhu is a haven where luxury reigns supreme. Featuring upscale comfort with lavish rooms and suites, they also provide an elaborate dinner and breakfast buffet at Lotus Café. Fun family activities are also on the cards for you to spend quality time with your kids.

JW Marriott Mumbai Sahar

Located in Andheri, the hotel stands out for its rooms, a rejuvenating spa, and a top-of-the-line fitness centre. They also house a poolside lounge area with cabanas – a picturesque place to unwind. Relish some authentic Italian,Japanese and Indian food with the complimentary dinner and breakfast at JW café.

The Westin Mumbai Garden City

With a landscaped swimming pool and a posh restaurantoverlooking panoramic views of the Mumbai skyline, the Westin could be your next getaway. You can indulge in a delicious complimentary breakfast and dinner at the buffer. Discounts on laundry, food and beverages and car rentals are also available.

Renaissance Mumbai Convention Centre Hotel

Nestled on 15 acres, adorned with lush gardens and scenic walkways, the hotel is located on the picturesque shores of Lake Powai. Enjoy a chef-curated special dinner menu and complimentary breakfast at the serene Lake View Cafe. Make a booking at the spa, go to the gym, and end your day with a scrumptious Awadhi meal at Nawab Saheb restaurant!

Lakeside Chalet, Mumbai – Marriott Executive Apartments

A home away from home, these posh apartments face the beautiful Powai Lake are a no-brainer for people looking to get away from the hustle and bustle of the city. Offering a specially curated dinner menu and scrumptious breakfast at Lake View Café, Lakeside Chalet helps you indulge in a very personalised and sophisticated experience.

How does Marriott International Hotels make your stay special?

Along with amazing views, plush rooms and world-class facilities, Marriott International Hotels will also make your staycation too good to be true with deals and benefits you can't refuse.

● Savour a chef-curated special dinner menu.

● Enjoy a room upgrade to a suite subject to availability and give yourself an opportunity to experience epicurean delights at their best.

● With a flexible check-in and check-out facility, you can plan your staycation with ease.

● Relish complimentary breakfast while you fill your plates at the luscious restaurant buffet.

● Kids below 12 get a complimentary stay and dining facility.

The hotels also provide one complimentary activity for your kids like baking and pottery class to intrigue their creative minds.

You can also enjoy additional benefits as a Marriott Bonvoy member by accumulating bonus points for your future stays at their hotels. A highly awarded loyalty program means you can plan your next vacation before the current one ends!

The list of hotels and what’s on offer doesn’t just end here! To know more about Marriott Bonvoy’s ongoing deals and explore their other properties, visit their official website.

So what are you waiting for? It's time to finally take a break from your exile and get back to travel. But don’t just get back, get back with a bang with these surreal staycations by Marriott International Hotels. Enjoy this perfect blend of luxury, style, and comfort without the hassle of long travel or hours of planning.

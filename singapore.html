Your Best Travel Moments Can Win Ultimate Travel Experiences& Exciting Prizes From Apollo Tyres

By kadambari Bhatt

In a world of corporate ladders and preset career paths, it’s easy to agree that blending business and travel is a unique combination for anyone to pursue. While it may seem quirky and different from the conventional route, there’s a new reality show called The Apprentice: ONE Championship Edition that is breaking the barriers of professional possibilities and bringing this concept to your television screens.

The Apprentice: ONE Championship Edition is a fun, exciting and stirring show set in Singapore, a destination we all know is sought after by all travellers and those seeking work opportunities alike! The winner will take away a US $250,000 job offer with ONE and an opportunity to work in Singapore for one year! With a one of a kind opportunity on line to travel to Singapore and build a career in the business industry, this show is a dreamlike amalgamation!

ith a well-established business culture and picturesque locales, Singapore makes for the perfect destination to host this one-of-a-kind show. Away from the formulaic shows we are used to seeing, ‘The Apprentice: ONE Championship Edition’ will demand its participants to bring forward theiS as a business person and as an athlete. Sounds interesting, right?

The Apprentice: ONE Championship Edition – a combination of business and physical prowess

The Apprentice: ONE Championship Edition is all about courage, a true passion for entrepreneurship and the fire to do something outside the boundaries of a 9-5 job. If you’ve ever contemplated leaving your corporate job to follow your passions, then this show will surely provide the inspiration you need to travel and to follow your dreams. Stepping out of your comfort zone to create an empire and leaving your home is what exactly this show demands from the contestants! If you are thrilled by new places and ambitions that build a fortune,Singapore is the perfect location for both travellers and go-getters!

Based on values like hard work, compassion and integrity, this 13-episode reality show features 16 talented contestants from all over the world. The show is being hosted by the chairman and CEO of ONE Championship, Mr Chatri Sityodtong. Contestants are being taken to the edge of their physical and intellectual ability, out of which only one will win a whopping US $250,000 job offer to be Mr Chatri Sityodtong’s protégé for a year. What does this offer entail? Well, this is a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to help grow the US $1 billion media and sports empire that is ONE.

A star-studded line-up

As the contestants battle it out for the grand prize, the show will host 12 special guests who are successful billionaires and CEOs of some of the world’s top companies such as Zoom and Grab. In addition to this, 12 mixed martial arts champions and legends will step in to push the candidates to their breaking points. Some noteworthy names to expect are Georges St-Pierre, Demetrious Johnson and Ben Askren.

Discover Singapore from your home

Since we are uncertain about when we can travel freely again, the show is the perfect way to stay home and discover some iconic locations in Singapore. Explore Singapore’s exotic outdoor and indoor offerings in these 13 episodes. Watch the show and admire bucket-list-worthy locations such as Jewel, Singapore Zoo, Ocean Restaurant, Fort Siloso Skywalk, Little India, Chinatown, Orchard Road, Kampong Glam, Clarke Quay, Joo Chiat, Gardens by the Bay and more!

Join the action and support Indian contestant Niraj Puran Rao

It’s a proud moment for the country as one of the 16 participants is an Indian – Niraj Puran Rao. Winner of the 2017 edition of Asli Champion, Niraj is a former professional football player with an MBA in Sports Management. He is also the founder of JOATFIT, an online fitness and health company. His strong business acumen and proven track record in the field of sports makes him a strong contender for the top spot.

Niraj will be competing with other candidates from countries all over the world, such as Singapore, Indonesia, Russia, New Zealand, Germany, the Philippines, Venezuela, the United States, Japan and Thailand.

How can you watch the show?

While some episodes of the show have already premiered in some Asian countries such as China, South Korea, Vietnam and Thailand, expect The Apprentice: ONE Championship Edition to be aired on Republic TV from March 20 till June 12, 2021.

The show promises an action-packed rollercoaster and I can’t wait to watch the first episode. Get ready to be shocked, thrilled and entertained as The Apprentice: Once Championship Edition prepares to blow your minds!
